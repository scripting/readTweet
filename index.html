Let's get your email address

This is for people who use Drummer.

The purpose of this app is to get your email address from your Twitter account.

Once we have this info, we can copy the outlines from your old Drummer account to the new one.

How to:

  • In Twitter create a new tweet containing only the text of your email address.
  • Copy the URL of the tweet to the clipboard.
  • Come back to this page, and paste the URL of the tweet in the edit box below. Click Go.
  • Our server will read the tweet, and if it correctly finds the email address, will record it in our database.
  • You can then delete the tweet. It isn't needed any longer.
  • If there's an error, it will tell you what the error is. ;-)
